How to rent a car with us

Renting a car with AZoom Car Rental is a simple and convenient process. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Select your vehicle of choice and reserve it
  2. Collect your vehicle from one of our rental offices
  3. Drive to your heart's content
  4. Return the vehicle to a designated carpark
  5. The vehicle will be inspected to ensure no damage occurred during the rental period
  6. Payment will be processed via credit card
